Reading Lists
Iconic Series About Life in Restaurants
Working at a restaurant has its ups and downs, but it can be very rewarding. From preparing the meals to waiting tables, everything is both exciting and stressful. Have a seat and enjoy the funny restaurant stories in this collection!
Manga About Powerful But Outdated Immortals
These hermits have spent a long time in seclusion, whether it be to study something, train, or just sleep. What happens when they finally come out of hiding and enter the modern world? Follow these powerful people as they rediscover the world and change it with their immense abilities and skills.
Comedy Centers Around Naive Protagonists
It's important to be aware of your surroundings, so you don't put yourself into harm. But the main characters in these manga series don't have awareness, experience or judgment to avoid dangerous and silly situations around them. Follow these innocent protagonists get themselves into funny and goofy conditions!
The Battles of Sweet Soldier Girls
When you see moe and adorable girls, you think of them wearing cute clothes, being innocent, and occasionally they are helpless on their own. But sometimes, they can be great candidates to be soldiers in the military. Follow these cute girls with guns and firearms be placed in the front lines.
Badass Female Leads
Male protagonists are common in seinen manga to cater to older male audiences. But what if there is a seinen manga that instead of a male main character, it's a female main character? Impossible? It's possible! And this collection of seinen manga showcases female leads with cool and awesome personalities.
Relaxing Manga to End the Day!
Sometimes we need to take a break from the hectic life. Join the sweet characters in this collection in their peaceful daily lives and relax!
The Age Of New Team's Ace
A new generation of athletes has appeared, and they are set to change the game entirely as they bring in new talent and skill. Supernatural abilities and intense matches take the readers on a sports adventure like no other.
